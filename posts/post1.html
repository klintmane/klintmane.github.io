Phew, what a relief!

---------------------------

09/07/2016 by Klint

Hopefully I am going to say this only once :

- I have finally found a home for my online presence!

Starting with a clean slate is nice, but unless you really follow through, it is always going to be a huge mess. I’ve had a website, I’ve had a blog, I’ve had startup domains but I have always felt somehow disconnected and now I think I will be combining all of them in one place (this site).

The reason I am doing this is because I want to introduce my defragmented self. I want to give people who stumble upon this site a way to get to know me, personally and professionally. I want to share my projects, skills and maybe my story and personal thoughts. So this basically is an outlet of some sorts and kind of the official one.

Thank you,
